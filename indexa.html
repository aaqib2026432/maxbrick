About Us

A kiln built on trust

MAX Brick Kiln, Brick MAX, produces traditionally fired clay bricks from our yard in Kultreh, Chadoora. We work with builders, contractors, and homeowners across the Kashmir valley, supplying bricks straight from the kiln for construction of every scale.

From raw clay to the finished stack, every step is handled on site — moulding, drying, and firing — so you get bricks that are consistent, well-fired, and ready for your next build.
